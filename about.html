About Our Club

Our Story

Redefining Fitness in Himachal Pradesh

Founded with a vision to bring world-class fitness infrastructure to the beautiful town of Chamba, Core Energy Fitness Club has evolved into a premier destination for athletic training and physical transformations.

We believed that the people of Chamba deserved a facility that rivals the best gyms in major metros. By merging state-of-the-art strength training equipment, highly certified personal coaching, and structured dietary guidance, we created a motivating environment where anyone can achieve their physical potential.

Whether you are a beginner taking your first steps into weightlifting or a seasoned athlete aiming for advanced conditioning, our community is built to support, inspire, and drive you forward.

Our Purpose

Mission & Vision

Our Mission: To empower the community of Chamba to live healthier, stronger, and more energetic lives by providing top-tier training facilities, evidence-based training methods, and authentic guidance.

Our Vision: To be recognized as the gold standard of fitness in Himachal Pradesh—not just as a place to sweat, but as a hub for real, sustainable body transformations, scientific training, and mental resilience.

The Facility

Premium Luxury Amenities

We invest in premium-grade equipment and clean, modern features to ensure your training session is seamless, safe, and elite.

Olympic Lifting Decks

Perform heavy compound deadlifts and squats on certified rubber platforms featuring professional Eleiko bars and plates.

Functional Turf Zone

Designed for dynamic movements: battle ropes, kettlebell circuits, plyo boxes, and sled-push tracks on high-traction grass turf.

Locker & Shower Rooms

Clean, secure electronic lockers and luxury showers to refresh immediately after an intense high-energy training session.

Himalayan View Cardios

A full row of modern touch-screen treadmills and air-bikes overlooking the clean, gorgeous peaks of Chamba.
